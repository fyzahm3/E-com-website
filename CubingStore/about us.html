About Ernő

Ernő is named after Ernő Rubik, the brilliant mind behind the iconic Rubik's Cube. Our company was founded with a passion for quality, innovation, and the timeless joy of puzzle-solving.

Our Mission

At Ernő, we are dedicated to providing high-quality cubes, shape-mods, and accessories to cubing enthusiasts around the world. Whether you're a beginner or a speedcuber, we strive to bring you the best products and an unmatched shopping experience.

Our Journey

Founded in 2024, Ernő was created by cube lovers for cube lovers. Our goal has always been to deliver top-tier puzzles and accessories while fostering a community that shares our passion for problem-solving.
